The 13th official European Yoyo Championship will be held in Trnava, Slovakia on the weekend of December 5th-7th 2025.
EYYC is the premier event of the European yoyo scene, where the best players from across the continent and beyond will compete across multiple divisions to claim the title of European Yo-yo Champion. See you there!

Admission

    Spectators ticket

      Spectators will be able to purchase entry tickets at the venue.

    Competitor fee

    1. Before November 1st: 50€
    2. Before November 24th: 80€
    3. Final Wave: 100€
      4. Competitor fee includes standard venue admission

      Every additional division entered is distcounted to 25€.
      e.g. First wave registration 1A + 4A + Junior division contestant will pay 100€
